At the test centre

You must:

  1. get to the test centre 15 minutes before the test starts
  2. bring your driving licence
    (If you have a paper licence, bring an in-date passport too)

If you do not bring your licence, we will cancel your test. We will not give you a refund.

Security

The test centre staff will check for items that you could use to cheat. They might also ask you to take off outer clothing or accessories.

We will cancel your test if you do not let them check.

It is against the law to cheat in a theory test. You can go to prison and get a driving ban.

Personal items

You must:

  • turn off your phone before going into the test centre
  • put all personal items in a locker

If you cannot fit your personal items in the locker or we have them in the test room, we might cancel the test and ask you to leave. We will not give you a refund.

Find out what you can take to your test at: www.gov.uk/theory-test/what-to-take
