Do you need support while taking the driving test?

Support is available during the driving test for people who have a disability, health condition or difficulty reading.

Depending on the type of test, this might include:

  • an English or Welsh voiceover
  • use of British Sign Language
  • extra time to complete the test
  • someone reading or rewording the questions

We may ask for supporting evidence from a medical or educational professional.

You can learn more about the types of support available.
