About this test centre

Telephone: 0800 093 4520

Description

The test centre entrance is located off Dale End opposite NCP Londonderry House and adjacent to the law court areas. There is no access through the McLaren Building.

Waiting facilities

There are no waiting facilities for any adults or children accompanying candidates.

Accessibility (building)

Disabled access is available As you approach the front door for the test centre, turn right towards the bridge. Before you go under the bridge there is a ramp on your left.

The main entrance to the McLaren building is at the top of the ramp on the left. Use the entrance bell to alert staff who will arrange access.

Accessibility (driving test)

Support available at this location:

  • Voiceover
  • British Sign Language

Welsh language

Tests can be presented in Welsh at this location.
