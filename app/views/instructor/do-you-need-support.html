Support during the driving test

You can get support during the driving test if you have:

  • a disability
  • a health condition
  • or have difficulty reading

You can ask for:

  • A voiceover with headphones
  • Bristish sign language on the screen
  • Extra time to do the test
  • Someone to read the questions for you
  • Have the wording of the questions made easier to read
  • A BSL interpreter

If you need support, you might be asked to provide a letter from a teacher or a doctor.
