Get the right support during your driving test

We want you to have everything you need to take your test.

Many types of support are available if, for example, you:

  • find reading difficult
  • have a disability
  • have a health condition

You can learn more about the types of support available. These include:

  • voiceover with headphones
  • British Sign Language
  • extra time to complete your test
  • someone reading the questions for you
  • having the questions worded differentlyrequest

You may be able to select the support you want and book your test online now.

Alternatively, you can tell us you want support and we'll get in touch with you.

This may suit you better because we'll be able to:

  • talk to you about how we can help
  • make any special arrangements you might need
  • talk about whether we need any evidence to support your request
